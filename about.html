Bio

I'm a final-year B.Tech student in Electronics and Communication Engineering at NIT Puducherry (CGPA 9.75/10), currently a Summer Research Intern at IIT Madras, working on transistor and circuit characterization in TSMC 180nm and PTM-MG 20nm FinFET technologies.

My core interest is analog and mixed-signal IC design — I've taken two chips through tapeout on the SCL 180nm PDK, and spend most of my time in Cadence Virtuoso debugging things at the transistor level rather than staying purely at the architecture level.

I also work across the embedded/FPGA stack — Arduino, ESP32, Raspberry Pi, and FPGA boards like Basys 3 and the PolarFire Icicle Kit — and have carried a few of those projects through to published work.
