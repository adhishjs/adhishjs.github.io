Overview

Industrial Telecommunication Exposure

Gained practical exposure to communication technologies such as VSAT, GSM, and SCADA systems at ONGC's Cauvery Asset. Bridged the gap between theoretical knowledge and real-world applications in the oil and gas industry, enhancing skills in monitoring and control technologies essential for industrial processes.

  • Learned about satellite communication (VSAT C-Band), GSM architecture, and Radio (PMP 5G Force 200) systems.
  • Studied Telephone Exchange (CORAL IP based) and fundamental networking concepts (LAN, WAN, VPN, IP addressing).
  • Explored SCADA systems used for real-time data acquisition, process control, and industrial automation.
  • Understood the importance of communication infrastructure in ensuring safety and efficiency in oil & gas operations.

Key Learnings

VSAT Communication

Understood the configuration of VSAT (ODU & IDU), signal attenuation factors, and the use of C-Band (4 GHz downlink / 6 GHz uplink) in ONGC's star topology network with hub at Delhi.

GSM & Radio

Studied GSM architecture (MS, BSS, NSS, OSS), routers, and the deployment of PMP 5G Force 200 for reliable point-to-point wireless broadband connectivity.

SCADA Systems

Monitored real-time data acquisition and process control, learning about communication protocols like HART, MODBUS, and Foundation Fieldbus used in industrial automation.

Telephony & Networking

Gained hands-on experience with CORAL IP based exchange, Krone modules, and fundamental networking concepts including IP addressing (IPv4 classes), subnetting, and pinging.
